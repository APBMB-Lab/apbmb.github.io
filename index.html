Welcome to the APBMB Research Lab

About Us

The APBMB Research Lab is dedicated to advancing the field of bioinformatics through innovative research projects. Our primary focus is on enhancing crops such as wheat and sesame using bioinformatics methodologies.

Our Research

Our research interests include microbial diversity analysis using high-throughput sequencing (HTS), machine learning, and statistical analysis. We have a strong background in metabolic modeling from microbial genomic data.
